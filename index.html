Maximizing Influence With Minimal Resources

We consider two different types of influence model, Connectivity and Casade, to determine the best choice of 5 people that maximizes the influence to local community through their network.

Connectivity

This model use only the first degree connection of each person to model influences.

Live View

Casade

This model use only both the first degree connection and the (discounted) second degree connection to model influences.
